Welcome! To get started, choose a character using the left and right keys and hit Enter.

Use the left, right, up and down keys to move your character around the screen. Try to avoid the bugs and make it to the stone square at the top of the screen.

Press the q key to restart the game at any time.

Try to pick up the stars for a higher score! If a heart appears, grab it for an extra life!

Thanks for playing, and have fun!
